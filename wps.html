About WPS

Weekly Productive Sessions (WPS) is an initiative by Think-Digital with the aim of reaching out to students across India to help them understand their interests, expand their horizons and inform them about the different opportunities available to them in the coming years.


WPS is a 2 month long bootcamp where students from all across India will be mentored on topics in the IT industry, such as

  • Full Stack Web Development
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Internet of Things
  • Machine Learning

In case you are less inclined towards the technical aspects we also provide guidance on Non-Technical Domains such as

  • Graphic Design
  • Content Writing

FAQ

What are the different payment options available?

Payments will be made through razorpay.

When can I change my subscriptions?

You can upgrade to the paid plans or downgrade to the free plans at anytime you want, irrespective of which plan you are currently on.

How frequently will I be billed?

Billing is based on the plan you choose. You will be billed monthly for the monthly plan and once per year for the yearly plan. The free plan has no cost.

Do I have to provide my account details for the free plan?

No, we do not collect any account/card details for the free plan.

Is there a trial period for the paid plan?

No there isn't, instead you could opt for the free plan, if you need more services then upgrade to the monthly payment plan

Can I use Data Atlus live chat to multiple websites?

Yes it's possible to use Data Atlus live chat on multiple websites/stores, however please note that only one bonfire can be created, irrespective of the plan. This bonfire can be further added to multiple sites.

Can I choose which feature to upgrade?

It's not possible to upgrade features individually, but upgrading to the monthly plan and paying for what you need, when you need it, can be a better option. In case of additional assitance feel free to contact us.
